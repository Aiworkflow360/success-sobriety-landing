## Technology

Innovation you can see, hear, and feel.

### Pushing boundaries from the inside out.

Spatial experiences on Apple Vision Pro are only possible through groundbreaking Apple technology. Displays that deliver more pixels than a 4K TV to each eye. Incredible Spatial Audio. A revolutionary dual‑chip design featuring the powerful M5 chip. A sophisticated array of cameras and sensors. All the elements work together to create an unprecedented experience you have to see to believe.

![Rear view of Apple Vision Pro showing the displays](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/displays/hero_base__bpxhq09r962u_large.jpg)

### More pixels than a 4K TV. For each eye.

The custom micro‑OLED display system features 23 million pixels, delivering stunning resolution and colors. And a specially designed three‑element lens creates the feeling of a display that’s everywhere you look.

![Advanced Spatial Audio analyzes the room you’re in](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/spatial_audio_startframe__dl2iakzba36u_large.jpg)![Advanced Spatial Audio analyzes the room you’re in](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/spatial_audio_base__d8zvuse3yu4i_large.jpg)

### Our most advanced Spatial Audio system ever.

Dual-driver audio pods positioned next to each ear deliver personalized sound while letting you hear what’s around you. Spatial Audio makes sounds feel like they’re coming from your surroundings. Audio ray tracing analyzes your room’s acoustic properties to adapt and match sound to your space. And if you want to use headphones with Apple Vision Pro, AirPods Pro 3 and AirPods 4 offer the perfect experience — featuring Lossless Audio with ultra-low latency, supported by the H2‑to‑H2 connection across devices.

![](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/features/eye_tracking_off__fx6m2dj3mlqq_large.jpg)![Inside view of Apple Vision Pro highlighting the LEDs and infrared cameras that enable precision eye-tracking](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/features/eye_tracking_on__ln11reqs6mi6_large.jpg)

### Responsive, precision eye tracking.

A high‑performance eye‑tracking system of LEDs and infrared cameras projects invisible light patterns onto each eye. This advanced system provides ultraprecise input, so you can accurately select elements just by looking at them.

### A sophisticated sensor array.

A pair of high-resolution cameras transmit over one billion pixels per second to the displays so you can see the world around you clearly. The system also helps deliver precise head and hand tracking and real‑time 3D mapping, all while understanding your hand gestures from a wide range of positions.

![Technical, exploded view inside Apple Vision Pro highlighting the M5 and R1 chips](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/technology/features/sensors_chips__s805s5o3gkii_large.jpg)

### Revolutionary dual‑chip performance. Now powered by M5.

Apple Vision Pro makes a giant leap in chip performance with the new M5. It simultaneously runs visionOS, executes advanced computer vision algorithms, and delivers stunning graphics. And the R1 chip is specifically dedicated to process input from the cameras, sensors, and microphones, streaming images to the displays within 12 milliseconds — for a virtually lag‑free, real‑time view of the world. With the extra power and efficiency provided by M5, Apple Vision Pro delivers even more incredible spatial experiences.

## Values

Designed to make a difference.

### Our values lead the way.

Apple Vision Pro was designed to help protect your privacy and keep you in control of your data. Its built‑in accessibility features are designed to work the way you do.

- ![](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/values/drawer/optic_startframe__bgyqs4wyl82q_large.jpg)













Use Optic ID to authorize purchases and unlock passwords with the uniqueness of your iris. Optic ID data is private and encrypted, and it never leaves your device. It’s accessible only to the Secure Enclave processor.

- ![Icon of an upheld hand inside a circle](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/values/drawer/camera_sensor_data__ed5zeclvc7qu_large.jpg)





Data from cameras and sensors is processed at the system level, so individual apps don’t need to see your surroundings to enable spatial experiences.

- ![](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/values/drawer/eye_input_startframe__csxoocg1wu82_large.jpg)













Eye input is not shared with Apple, third-party apps, or websites. Only your final selections are transmitted when you tap your fingers together.

- ![Icon of a human figure with widespread arms inside a circle](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/values/drawer/accessibility__fkae0052zl26_large.jpg)





Inclusive by design, Apple Vision Pro comes with built-in features like Smart Color Invert, Reduce Transparency Support, Cochlear Implant Support, Live Captions,[13](https://www.apple.com/apple-vision-pro/#footnote-13) and Live Recognition, which can describe surroundings, find objects, and read documents in real time.

- ![Grey Apple logo with green leaf](https://www.apple.com/v/apple-vision-pro/j/images/overview/values/drawer/recycled__yesh19ceo6au_large.jpg)





Apple Vision Pro contains 100% recycled aluminum in the frame and battery enclosure.
